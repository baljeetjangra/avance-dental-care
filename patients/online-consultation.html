Consult Our Dentist from anywhere!

Avance Dental Care has been the preferred choice for several families and patients for quality dental care and advice. Now you can consult with Best dentist sitting back home by booking E- Consultation.

Also, the Avance Dental Care Second Opinion E-Consultation service is available for patients who would like to have an online consultation to confirm the diagnosis and treatment plan recommended to them by their previous dentist. This second opinion is assessed by a group of renowned dental specialists.

Be Sure, Get a Second Opinion

To request an online dental Second Opinion, you must fill a detailed priority consultation form. Once all of the required medical and past dental records are received and found to be complete, your case will be assigned to a group of specialist who carry out a thorough discussion. Post discussion a review is provided in the form of a comprehensive report in approximately 7 business days.

Please note that all online submissions of medical and dental records pertaining to your Online Dental Second Opinion must be translated to English prior to being submitted. For more details call +91-9878300016
