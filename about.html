Our Core Dental Team

Avance Dental Care started in 2013 as a joint effort of a husband and wife trying to make ends meet and practice quality dentistry after years of dental practice as consultants at various centers. The couple had varied professional expertise. A Prosthodontist & Implantologist married to Conservative & Restorative Dentist - so that the diagnosis and treatment plan they made together often bore the tool marks of true and ethical dentistry. An Exclusive implants and restorative practice had the vision to provide best in class care for dental implants and restorative dentistry.

The family practice soon became a state of art multispecialty practice with a team of dental specialist and professionals who worked with a vision to provide best in class dental care equipped with latest technological advancements. Today the team of well qualified dental professionals follows and complies with the stringent European standards of sterilization to ensure safety, cleanness and trust of the customers.

Our team is highly proficient in their approach and is always supervised to ensure optimum quality of services to our patients. The experience and qualifications of our dental care experts coupled with use of latest innovation and technology. The professional approach devoted in restoring and enhancing the natural beauty with high quality dental procedures result in long-lasting beautiful smile of our patients.
