Laser Dentistry – No Need To Worry!

Modern Diode lasers (Dental Laser) have successfully made Root canal therapy go high-tech and virtually pain free procedure possible in one visit. In Laser Dentistry, an intense parallel beam of light used to clean Root Canals, melts away “Smear layer” (Consisting of Bacteria and Infection) in root canals. Studies have found that lasers dramatically reduce the number of E. Faecalis (Bacteria) responsible for failure of a Root Canal Therapy.

For Sensitive Teeth
The problem of tooth sensitivity can be permanently eliminated with the help of laser Dentistry. The high intensity beam of light closes the thermo sensitive tubules giving a complete relief from tooth sensitivity.
Role in Packet Removal/Gum Therapy
Lasers at different wavelengths have provided supportive treatment in all aspects of periodontal problems. It is helpful in removal of diseased intra-pocket epithelium, bacteria and correction of bony pockets.
Biostimulation / Tissue Regeneration
The word “bio-stimulation” means life stimulation. Dental Laser therapy stimulates re-growth of bone, blood, muscles, hair cells and peripheral nerves. With most advanced lasers at Avance Dental Care, Bio-Stimulation has initiated faster healing of extraction sockets, wounds, pockets in healthy as well as severely diabetic patients and immuno-compromised patients. Lasers have been scientifically proven to Promote faster healing in Dental Implants.
