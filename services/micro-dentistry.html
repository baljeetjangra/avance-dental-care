Micro Dentistry – A boon for RCT (Root Canal Therapy)!

Microscope has become an essential part of practice at Avance Dental Care with ability of multiple zooming (Upto 22 times the size). The rule for modern dentistry at Avance Dental Care is “We don’t treat anything which we don’t see”. With help of micro dentistry “We see more and cure better”.

We can detect the leakage between the tooth and matrix (Band for Tooth Restoration) with this equipment and avoid possible tooth treatment complications. Microscopes are the biggest assets in the Root Canal Therapy. The minutest and most complex structures in the tooth and RCT (Root Canal Treatment) are now visible, thus moving away from uncertainty. Microscopes along with Dental Lasers jointly form a good recipe for successful Root Canal Therapy. Read in detail about RCT (Root Canal Therapy).

Microscope enables us to visualize and analyze any incorrectness in the tooth prosthetic jobs such as crowns and bridges. The perfect illumination of the field under microscope enables us to verify the cementation process of crowns and bridges. Extra cement which often has a detrimental effect on the periodontal tissues can be avoided. Microscopes are also helpful in retrieval of broken abutment screws, Complex Implant procedures, Effective indirect sinus lifts and treating Peri-implantitis in Dental Implantology.
