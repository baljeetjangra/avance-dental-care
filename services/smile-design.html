Teeth Whitening, Veneers and Smile Design

One tradition that is common across many societies in the world is “photography” for the wedding day. Irrespective of whom you are, all of us want to look good. The most innovative and effective techniques to make your wedding day photographs special and memorable are as below.

Dental Cleaning

To get unwanted stains removed and freshen the breath, Professional Dental Cleaning is best advice. It is the best way to have effective bacterial plaque control.

  • Procedure duration:
    45 minutes to 60 minutes.
  • Anesthesia:
    On most occasions local anesthetic is required but conscious sedation and general anesthesia options are available.
  • Postoperative checkups are required every 3 to 6 months.
  • Shelf life:
    You may require a repeat cleaning after 6 to 8 months depending upon individual situation.
  • Healing time:
    None.
  • Possible Risks:
    None.

Teeth Whitening

A professional in office teeth whitening may brighten your smile.

  • Procedure duration:
    Usually takes 60 minutes in office teeth whitening. At home whitening may take 2 to 3 weeks.
  • Anesthesia:
    None
  • Postoperative checkups are required after every 6 months.
  • Shelf life:
    Results are depending upon individual situations and variables such as diet, tobacco, alcohol and actual tooth color.
  • Healing time:
    None
  • Possible Risks:
    Thermal sensitivity lasting for 2 to 4 days.

Veneers

Tooth Veneers are a great and effective way to change the shape and color of the teeth. They are thin aesthetic shells of the ceramic pasted over the tooth. The teeth usually require slide reduction in the outer enamel to incorporate veneers. You can let them work for wedding day photography.

  • Procedure duration:
    Depends upon number of teeth planned.
  • Anesthesia:
    Occasionally may require topical or local anesthesia.
  • Postoperative checkups are required after every 6 months.
  • Shelf life:
    Results usually last for 10 to 15 years depending upon individual dental maintenance.
  • Healing time:
    None
  • Possible Risks:
    Thermal sensitivity may occur in some situation.

Bonding

High-tech materials bond to the tooth and give the life like restorations. Old discolored filings, broken teeth can be repaired with bonding.

  • Procedure duration:
    Usually takes 45 minutes per tooth. However the total time may depend upon the number of teeth to be repaired.
  • Anesthesia:
    Occasionally may require local anesthesia.
  • Postoperative checkups are required after every 6 months.
  • Shelf life:
    Results are depending upon individual situations and variables such as diet, tobacco.
  • Healing time:
    None
  • Possible Risks:
    Chewing of hard food can damage the bonding.
