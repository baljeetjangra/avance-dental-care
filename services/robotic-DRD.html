Robotic Digital Restoration Designer (Robotic DRD)

We at Avance Dental Care continuously invest in research and future of modern dentistry. Robotic Digital Restoration Designer (Robotic DRD) with integrated solutions is one such initiative and has ushered a paradigm shift in the Dental Industry and promises excellent results for the patients enhancing precision, comfort and safety as compared to conventional (manual) restorative techniques by Dentists and Laboratory staff which inadvertently incorporated errors.

Robotic DRD has shown excellent results for Crowns, Restorations, Implant crowns and bridges, accurate implant positioning guides, orthodontic aligners, safer and less time consuming making it possible in Single Visit. Results with Robotic DRD are virtually unrivaled. Above all, it presents a special treatment experience for both dentists and patients.

Steps by Step Procedure

  1. Intra-oral SCAN in 60 Seconds
    Uses the most powerful Intra-oral camera systems with fluorescent technology that helps in the early identification of caries. It also helps in the identification of possible interproximal caries undetectable to eye.
  2. Designing in 2 Minutes
    The Robotic DRD generates automatic design proposals based on the Jaw function. This saves time and offers the final design of the restoration quickly.
  3. Prepare in 20 Minutes
    The Milling unit then prepares inlays, onlays, crowns, veneers, implant crowns, bridges and customized abutments. In addition, milling or grinding of zirconium oxide bridges, bars, attachments, and NPM restorations can also be done.

What Robotic DRD can do for you?

  • Restorations in a single visit:
    The system ensures complete control over workflows with High-performance material to make single-visit dentistry restorations possible.
  • Place implants safely and precisely:
    Robotic DRD not only enables us to create customized implant prosthetics, but also helps planning the surgery and insert the implant accurately and precisely. The prosthetic situation can be taken into account when planning the implant. This prosthetic-oriented planning ensures the implant properly positioned with the surgical guide milled in the office. We can also quickly treat the implant with a screw-retained crown on TiBases, with the highest degree of gingival biocompatibility.
  • Access to orthodontic treatment:
    We expanded our treatment range to include orthodontic applications. We now focus on orthodontics utilizing the advanced tools such as the integrated model analysis system. Once the treatment planning is complete, we export or send the case with a single click.
